# Public Results Boundary

## What the public outputs can support

The public outputs can support limited, source-boundary constrained statements such as:

- the pipeline separates strict battery records by operating and planned status
- the pipeline creates state-level summaries from EIA-860M records
- the workflow applies validation gates before public-facing claims
- the strict classifier excludes broader storage categories that are not batteries

## What the public outputs do not support

The public outputs do not support claims about:

- definitive U.S. battery market size
- final deployment totals
- investment opportunity
- policy effectiveness
- grid stress
- forecasts
- complete national coverage beyond the EIA-860M source boundary

## Required wording

Any numeric statement based on these outputs should use this wording:

"Based on a strict EIA-860M classifier output, not a final market-total estimate."
